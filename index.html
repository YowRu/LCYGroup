R&D Center: addressing sustainability trends through green solutions

LCY Chemical values innovation and talent development. At the heart of its competitive edge is the R&D center, which is committed to offering sustainable and circular economy-based products.

Vincent Liu x Alan Lin Dialogue:LCY Chemical Gains Edge on Circular Economy Amid Carbon Neutrality Wave

While carbon neutrality has become a global goal, it will present unprecedented challenges for the petrochemical industry. Alan Lin, Deputy Executive Director of the Office of Carbon Reduction, and Vincent Liu, General Manager of LCY Chemical, share their thoughts on how Taiwan can keep up with the green push and strive for the circular economy.

LCY X REnato lab Dialogue: Pursue Circular Economy for Sustainability

To mitigate climate change, the technology industry is putting greater emphasis on sustainability. Apple, for example, has pledged to be entirely carbon neutral by 2030. Taiwanese companies are also doing their part. LCY Chemical, LCY Technology, and REnato lab are committed to finding the best ways to use resources within a circular economy.

LCY R&D Center Cultivates Talent, Creativity and Business

LCY Chemical consistently introduces scientific innovations that surprise the world. The secret behind these achievements is an international team of top-tier R&D talents, based at the R&D Center in Nanzih, Kaohsiung, where their innovative energy can gather momentum. The R&D team puts the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship into practice, through creative ideas that incorporate market trends, yields patent applications, and puts products on the market, generating competitiveness that demands the attention of the world.
